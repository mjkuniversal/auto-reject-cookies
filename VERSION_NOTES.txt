Version 1.7.3 / Chrome 4.3 - mymdnow.com Support

New CMP Support:
- Added mymdnow.com cookie consent handler
- Site uses toggle checkboxes (no "Reject All" button) — extension unchecks optional cookie categories and clicks "Save Changes"
- Also added CUSTOM_CMP_HANDLERS infrastructure to Chrome build (was Firefox-only)

---

Notes to Reviewer:

No account required for testing.

To test mymdnow.com support:
1. Visit mymdnow.com
2. The extension should detect the cookie consent dialog, uncheck non-essential toggles, and click "Save Changes"
3. With DEBUG=true in firefox/content.js, console shows "MyMDNow: Unchecked toggle:" messages followed by "Rejected via MyMDNow"

The extension requires no external services or accounts - it operates entirely client-side by detecting and clicking cookie consent UI elements.
