Features

Automatic Rejection

Instantly clicks "Reject All" on cookie banners using smart pattern matching and CMP-specific selectors.

Global Privacy Control

Sends the GPC signal with every request, legally requiring sites to honor your privacy preferences in supported jurisdictions.

Multi-Language Support

Recognizes reject buttons in English, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Dutch, Portuguese, Polish, Swedish, Norwegian, and Danish.

Two-Step Rejection

Handles sites that hide reject options behind "Settings" or "Preferences" buttons by clicking through automatically.

25+ CMPs Supported

Works with OneTrust, Cookiebot, TrustArc, Quantcast, Didomi, and many more consent management platforms.

Zero Data Collection

Runs entirely in your browser. No accounts, no tracking, no data sent anywhere. Your browsing stays private.

How It Works

1

Page Loads

When you visit a website, the extension immediately injects the Global Privacy Control signal into the page and request headers.

2

Banner Detection

The extension scans for known consent management platforms and cookie banners using a comprehensive database of selectors.

3

Smart Rejection

It identifies and clicks "Reject All" buttons using pattern matching across 11 languages, always prioritizing full rejection over partial options.

4

Continuous Monitoring

A mutation observer watches for dynamically loaded banners, catching popups that appear after initial page load.
